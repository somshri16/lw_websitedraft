meet the team

Left Winger Designs, based in New Delhi, is a luxury brand specializing in interior architecture, furniture, and decor. Our bespoke designs are carefully crafted to align with our clients' unique visions, achieving a perfect blend of panache and elegance.

We strive to maintain a harmonious balance between modernity and simplicity, keeping our clients' preferences at the forefront.

With a manufacturing unit located in Delhi, Leftwinger Designs boasts a remarkable track record of completing over 60+ prestigious projects, Specializing in luxury interior design.

the company's 7+ years of experience span across India, Dubai, Australia, and the USA, establishing their expertise in delivering exceptional craftsmanship worldwide.
